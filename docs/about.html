Learn more about Grup!

Grup is the work of four talented individuals for their University of Washington Bothell CSS 385 Game Design class. We started with a piece of art and an idea and ran with it. We knew that we wanted the game to be laid back, relaxed, and casual. We wanted a small challenge on the platforming levels and a nice visual reward when the player clears the pollutants.

We also wanted to make a statement, that even the smallest actions can have an impact, especially when it comes to the environment. That is why each pollutant cleared returns color to the neighboring area. Grup's world is not huge, but Grup's actions make an impact in their world.
